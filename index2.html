Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

How does your AI-powered matching work?

Our proprietary AI analyzes 500+ data points including skills, experience, cultural preferences, communication style, and performance history. It cross-references these with your company's requirements, culture, and success patterns to generate highly accurate match scores.

What's your average time to placement?

Our average time to placement is 24 hours for temporary positions and 7-14 days for permanent roles. For executive positions, we typically deliver qualified candidates within 2-3 weeks. Our AI-powered matching significantly reduces the traditional recruitment timeline.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes! We offer comprehensive replacement guarantees: 90 days for temporary placements, 180 days for permanent hires, and 1 year for executive positions. If a candidate doesn't work out within the guarantee period, we'll provide a replacement at no additional cost.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing, E-commerce, Clean Energy, Education, and Creative industries. Our team includes specialists with deep domain expertise in each sector, ensuring we understand the unique requirements and challenges of your industry.

How do you ensure candidate quality?

Our multi-stage vetting process includes AI-powered screening, technical assessments, behavioral interviews, reference checks, and background verification. We also conduct cultural fit analysis and predictive performance modeling to ensure long-term success.
